Digital Based Exchange

The Gateway to a Monetary Revolution

DBEX is not following a traditional path of launching a cryptocurrency exchange. We are not solely driven by the business aspects of this industry but rather by the process of sustainability that should increase cryptocurrencies adoption into mainstream commerce in the years to follow.

Harnessing the power of Blockchain technology DBEX will be a secure cryptocurrency exchange that will look to become the standard for tokens to be listed. This will be done through in-depth due diligence procedures, qualitative and quantitative research into the company launching their token. These procedures will be carried out in-house and by independent external experts.

The DBEX wallet will be a private digital wallet which users can use to store cryptocurrencies and ERC20 tokens. Users of the DBEX wallet be able to store their funds safely by following the security feature recommendations on the digital wallet. This wallet will be made available on mobile platforms such as iOS and Android.

FAQ

What is DBEX?

DBEX is short for Digital Based Exchange. We are cryptocurrency trading platform as well as a digital wallet for storing cryptocurrency.

What makes DBEX better than other exchange platforms?

DBEX is a cryptocurrency trading platform and a wallet. This is to make the conversion and storage of cryptocurrency convenient. DBEX aims to become the standard for token listings. We offer many other services that other exchanges ignore and we are always seeking to offer users the best trading experience.
The DBEX core team members stem from the same tertiary institution and have similar long-term visions to see this platform grow.  

What time zone do you operate in?

DBEX operates according to the South African Standard Time (SAST) zone. The time zone is two hour ahead of UTC (UTC +2).
